Porygon

By Grace and Nox


Summary

Why we chose this art

We like pokemon and porygon is made of geometrical shapes. Porygon is made of several triangles and rectangles; Porygon is one of the hundreds of pokemon you can find in the fictional world of pokemon.

How we coded it

We used the glide function to draw lines and shapes that is based on coordinates and positioning, that was used with several other sprites to create several shapes at once that connect to make the porygon.
